      Talent Acquisition Partner at Kuaishou

      Job Description

      1. Build, coordinate and improve the music operations strategy for short video platform.
      2. Develop strategic relationships with music partners across the region.
      3. Excecute and analyze music initiatives.

      4.Discover, support, analyze trending music, and update the music content ecosystem.

      5.Work closely with product operations to develop product-level tools to improve the efficiency of music promotion operation and analysis.


      Requirements

      1. 3+ years of experience in Content Operation or Music related industry. Short video platform working experience is preferred.
      2. Preferably you have good relationships throughout the music industry.
      3. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills with ability to take initiative and build productive relationships.
      4. Attention to detail and comfortable in multitasking environment.

      5.  Passion for music, pop culture and industry connections.

      6.  In-depth understanding of data analytical skills. Understanding of short video traffic data is preferred.

      7. Strong computer and data reporting skills.

      8. Experience working with international teams at different time zones.

      9. English and Spanish speaking.
